BODL-Service: Using AI to Generate Cocktails

BODL-Service

BODL stands for "Bartending Originality with Deep Learning". Deep learning and neural networks are relatively old, but recently exploding topics as they quickly surpass other methods of machine learning. I used this modified version of Andrej Karpathy's char-rnn along with TensorFlow and Flask to generate this site. I pulled my data from TheCocktailDB.

Recommend Usage

If you are not trying to make every cocktail you have the ingredients for, you just not doing it right. Good luck, have fun!
